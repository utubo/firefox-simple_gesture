# Privacy Policy

utubo ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Microsoft Edge extension "Simple Gesture" (the "Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use
The Extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, browsing history, or IP address) to any external servers.

## 2. Permissions Required
The Extension requests certain browser permissions solely to provide its core functionalities:
- Access recently closed tabs -> for restore closed tabs
- Access browser tabs -> for gestures to control tabs
- Access your data for all websites -> for to work on any websites
- Block content on any page -> PC site mode(User-Agent)
- Clipboard -> for custom gestues
- UserScripting -> for custom gestues

## 3. Data Security
Since we do not collect or store any personal data, your information remains entirely within your local device and browser environment.
